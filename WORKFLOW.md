
# Mapnshop App: The A-Z Workflow

This document outlines the complete operational workflow of the Mapnshop system, from initial setup to daily operations and reporting.

---

## **Part 1: Getting Started**

### **1. Authentication**
*   **Sign Up**: Create an account via Email/Password.
*   **Sign In**: Secure login to access your business dashboard.

### **2. Business Onboarding**
Upon first login, you are guided through a setup wizard:
1.  **Business Profile**: Name, phone, and category (Restaurant, Retail, Service, etc.).
2.  **Location**: Google-powered address search for precise location.
3.  **Operations**: Configure default currency, tax rates, and delivery fees.

### **3. Verification (Under Review)**
*   **Submission**: Once onboarded, your business enters a **Pending** state.
*   **Review Process**:
    *   `Request Submitted` ➔ `Verification in Progress` ➔ `Approval & Access`
*   **Status Check**: You can refresh your status or sign out while waiting for approval.

---

## **Part 2: The Core Loop (Daily Operations)**

### **1. Creating an Order (Any Source)**
Navigate to the **(+) Create** tab to log a new order from *any* channel.
*   **Source Selection**: 
    *   **Standard**: Manual, Phone, WhatsApp, Instagram, Walk-in.
    *   **Delivery Apps**: Select or type custom sources (e.g., *Uber Eats*, *DoorDash*, *Talabat*, *Just Eat*).
*   **Customer Entry**:
    *   **Smart Search**: Type a phone/name to find existing customers instantly.
    *   **New Customer**: Auto-saves details for future orders.
*   **Order Details**:
    *   **Delivery**: Switch on "Delivery Required" to use **Address Autocomplete**.
    *   **Financials**: Auto-calculated Subtotal, Tax, and Delivery Fees.

### **2. The Unified Inbox**
Navigate to the **Inbox** (Home) tab. This is your command center for *all* order sources.
*   **Visual Feed**: Orders from all channels (Manual, Uber, DoorDash) appear in one consistent list.
*   **Filtering**:
    *   **Status**: `Active`, `Completed`, `Cancelled`.
    *   **Source**: Filter by specific app (e.g., "Show only Uber Eats orders").
*   **Quick Actions**: One-tap status updates (Start, Ready, Complete) directly from the card.

### **3. Order Processing & Safety**
Tap any order to view the full **Order Details** screen.
*   **Safety First (Allergy Alerts)**:
    *   Prominent **Warning Banners** appear for orders with allergies or special instructions (e.g., "Gluten Free", "Nut Allergy").
*   **Workflow Pipeline**:
    *   `Created` ➔ `Preparing` ➔ `Ready` ➔ `Completed`
    *   **Undo**: Mistakenly changed status? Undo it immediately.
*   **Management**:
    *   **Timeline**: View a full audit log of every action (Status changes, Edits, Cancellations).
    *   **Edits**: Modify items or prices even after creation.
    *   **Attachments**: Upload photos (e.g., receipt, finished dish) as proof of work.
    *   **Internal Notes**: Add private team notes.

---

## **Part 3: Management & Insights**

### **1. Analytics (Reports)**
Navigate to the **Reports** tab.
*   **Daily Snapshots**: Revenue, Order Counts, AOV (Average Order Value).
*   **Performance**: Track Fulfillment Efficiency and Delivery vs. Pickup splits.
*   **Timeframes**: Today, Yesterday, Last 7 Days.

### **2. Settings & Team**
*   **Business Profile**: Edit hours, location, and contact info.
*   **Team Management**: Invite staff with specific roles (`Owner` vs `Staff`).
*   **Hardware**: Configure thermal printers.
*   **Security**: Manage password and session security.
